UX Case Study

Designing a high-converting digital marketing agency website

Research

User Interviews

Users wanted clarity, trust, and proof of results.

Competitor Analysis

Most agency websites are cluttered and confusing.

Key Insight

Trust and simplicity matter more than visuals.

Design

Created a minimal dark UI with gradient accents, clear CTAs, and structured information architecture.

Focused on reducing cognitive load and improving navigation flow.

Usability Testing

Task Completion

90% users completed booking flow successfully.

Issues Found

Users wanted clearer pricing section.

Improvement

Added stronger CTA visibility and social proof.

Final Outcome

Improved clarity, trust, and conversion-focused structure.
